About

// SWARMER

Low, heavy, ethereal, relentless progressive post-metal — Salt Lake City's SWARMER create a catharsis that bears repeating.

SWARMER is Chris Clement (Bird Eater, Dehorn), Jason Weidauer (Pilot This Plane Down, Dehorn), Peter Makowski (Form of Rocket, Accidente), and Andrew Drechsel (Bandhood, Laughter).
